# Logo Files

Please place your logo files in this folder:

- `henry-logo.png` - Henry company logo
- `enduraroof-logo.png` - Enduraroof company logo

These logos will appear at the top of the PDF quotes generated by the calculator.

**Recommended specifications:**
- Format: PNG (with transparent background preferred)
- Size: Approximately 400-600px wide
- Aspect ratio: Maintain original logo proportions
